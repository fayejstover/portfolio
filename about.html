About
Faye Stover

Product Designer

I design systems that make complex things feel simple. Currently a senior at the University of Michigan studying Information Science — focused on the intersection of research, clarity, and the aesthetics of how information moves.

My work spans B2B AI tooling (Intuit), consumer social (Roblox, LibbyU), and community design (Figma). I care about the moment a user goes from confused to confident.

I build fast with AI — Figma Make, v0, Claude Code — but always evaluate outputs as a user, not as the person who built it.
